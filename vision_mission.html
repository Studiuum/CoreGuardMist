OUR VISION & MISSION

Vision

Such a society where all of its people are empowered, confident and able to protect themselves in any situation to live freely without fear.

Mission

CoreGuard's mission is to provide people of all genders with effective, non-lethal security solutions that provide peace of mind without the hazards associated with firearms and other lethal weapons. We believe that everyone deserves to feel safe and confident, no matter where they are.
